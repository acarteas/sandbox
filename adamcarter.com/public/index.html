About Myself

I have a PhD in Computer Science from Washington State University and am employed as an Assistant Professor at Humboldt State University in Arcata, California. My research background is in data analytics, human-computer interaction, learning, and education. As a professor, I regularly teach some of our most demanding courses (e.g. data structures, algorithms) as well as our most cutting-edge courses (e.g. web development, mobile app development).

When working with clients, I follow the principles of user-centered design, ensuring that the final product delivered is the one that the client envisioned. My background as an educator exposes me to a diverse set of motivations, learning styles, and personalities. This experience has made me both an effective communicator and a good team player. I look forward to learning more about the details of your specific project.
